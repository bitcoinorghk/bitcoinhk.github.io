Join us after the Inside Bitcoins conference for The Bitcoin Association of Hong Kong’s official launch party in the heart of LKF!

Where

7th Heaven, 1/F, 17 Lan Kwai Fong, Central, Hong Kong

What’s on tap

Open bar from 7pm until 10pm for the first 100 guests courtesy of our friends at DigiPay! For everyone else, pay with Bitcoin and standard drinks are only HK$30 worth of Bitcoin each courtesy of CoinPip. Otherwise standard drinks are HK$50 each.
