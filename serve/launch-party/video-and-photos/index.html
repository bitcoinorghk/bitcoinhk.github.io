Launch Party Video & Photos

By +larry on 25 June, 2014

Thanks to every who came to our 24 June 2014 launch party after the Inside Bitcoin conference! We had over 200 people stop by to celebrate cryptocurrency on a Tuesday night!

It was great to see so many new faces, many of which were visiting Hong Kong for the first time

Thanks again to our sponsors DigiPay, ANX, CoinPip, and Ucan Brokerage and the hard work of our founding members without which this event couldn’t have happened!
